The discussion of [[Engineering Judgment in Balancing Accuracy and Effort]] reveals the fact that different companies/firms choose to approach the tradeoff differently as part of their competitive positioning strategy.

- Some firms want to convey that they have high technical competency, focus on [[Quality]], and minimize [[Risk and Uncertainty]] for clients.
- Some firms want to convey that they are the lowest-cost option.
