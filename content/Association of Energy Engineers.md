# Individual Membership Types
---

- Individual Member
	- An individual with an engineering, architectural, business, or law degree or a registered Professional Engineer or Architect.
- Affiliate Member
	- A person interested in the objectives of AEE who does not meet the criteria to be a full member.
- Individual Corporate Delegate
	- If your company is an AEE Corporate Member, you receive a special membership discount.
- Senior Member
	- A graduate of an engineering college or university or a registered Professional Engineer or Architect with six (6) or more years' experience in energy engineering or energy management.
- Young Energy Member
	- An individual 30 years of age or under with an engineering, architectural, business or law degree, or a registered Professional Engineer or Architect.
- Student Member
	- An individual who is a full-time matriculated student (must send evidence of full-time student status). Twelve credit hours for undergrad and nine credit hours for graduate students.
- Retired Member
	- An individual retired full-time who is no longer involved in any career activity.
- Lifetime Member
	- Open to all individuals, and includes lifetime subscriptions to all divisions.
