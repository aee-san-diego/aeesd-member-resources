# Training / AB 1234

In your career as an [[Energy Engineering|energy engineer]], you may serve on a public board or committee and be subject to California AB 1234. People subject to AB 1234 must complete at least two hours of ethics training every two years. This training covers general ethics principles and laws relevant to public service.
